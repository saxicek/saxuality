It is very simple:
  1. In /usr/syno/etc/packages/DownloadStation/download/settings.json edit item
    rpc-whitelist to IP you have. For example: "127.0.0.1,192.168.*.*"
  2. Get pid of your Transmission:
    ps w | grep transmission
  3. Send request of re-read configuration:
    kill -HUP
