While developing my Python app on Heroku I tried to connect to the shared instance of PostgreSQL but I failed to get SHARED_DATABASE_URL from the environment (used "heroku config" for the check). Neither DATABASE_URL was defined. Asked on the IRC and got the answer - I have to add it first. Here is the magic spell:

heroku addons:add shared-database:5mb
